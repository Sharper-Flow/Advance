`scripts/deploy-local.sh --fix` creates a timestamped backup of the global OpenCode config on every run, then on JSONC configs (the common case) silently skips the patch. Result: backups accumulate indefinitely (19 observed before cleanup) and JSONC drift produces only a warning with no actionable instructions and no non-zero exit. Fix: only backup when patching will actually happen, auto-prune to 3 most recent, and fail-loud with exact patch instructions when JSONC drift is detected.
