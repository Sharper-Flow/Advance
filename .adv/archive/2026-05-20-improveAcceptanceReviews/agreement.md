# Agreement

## Objectives
1. Improve scanability of acceptance-review summaries.
2. Add an executive summary to the review output shape.
3. Use ordered and nested lists for grouped remediation/details where helpful.
4. Keep the existing ADV gate handoff voice contract intact.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Acceptance review guidance/templates require an executive summary before detailed findings.
2. Delivered work and remediation details are rendered as structured ordered/nested lists where grouping improves readability.
3. The change updates the governing source of truth, not only an example output.
4. Nearby tests or asset checks cover the changed guidance/template behavior.

## Constraints
- Do not begin implementation during fast-track planning.
- Do not weaken acceptance review rigor or hide remediation details.
- Do not alter unrelated ADV gate voice surfaces.
