## Wisdom Accumulated

- **[pattern]** For ADV status latency work, OpenCode session-debt diagnostics are valuable as local DB/session archaeology but should not be part of summary or normal health hot paths. Confine expensive session-debt scans to hygiene/explicit doctor surfaces with freshness/degraded metadata; summary should not call scanOpenCodeSessionDebt.
